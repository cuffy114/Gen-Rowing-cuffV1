Term 3 Notes
Single Sculling

Majority of training at T2 (HR<165). Goal: how many laps each session — record on whiteboard.

Ergo

Broken 60′ @ T4 (≈80% of 2km power) with HR cap below 190bpm. Secondary sessions generally T2.

Mostly technical and T2. Power Strokes to develop force production. Threshold work added near end of term.

Race Prep

Transition to race pace. Maintain T2 base, add T5 sharpening. Race rehearsals.
